Nova 2084 is an original story in development by Black Espresso. It takes place in the year 2084 and revolves around the main character Nova, who discovers that society has been under the control of the mysterious, elitist government controlled by a megacorporation known as Iris Corp. By mandating super advanced Augmented Reality (AR) technology to be installed directly behind the eyes of citizens, this allows the government to blind and illude the people from ever seeing or being able to acknowledge the dark state of the world since after World War 3, and trick them into feeling as if they live within a perfect utopia despite being oppressed.
